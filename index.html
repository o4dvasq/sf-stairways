What's inside

Everything you need to explore SF on foot

  • 382 stairways across 53 neighborhoods Every public stairway in San Francisco, mapped and ready to explore.
  • Around Me mode Discover stairways nearby, sorted by distance. Great for a spontaneous walk.
  • Log walks, add photos, track progress Mark stairways as walked, attach photos, and watch your neighborhood map fill in.
  • Hard Mode Proximity-verified walks. You actually have to be there to log it.
  • Your data stays yours Syncs privately to iCloud. No account required. Nothing collected.

The story

Built by a 40-year San Francisco resident

I wanted to track the stairways I'd walked and discover the hundreds I hadn't. SF has over 350 public stairways tucked into its hills, each one a portal into a different neighborhood. Most people walk past them without knowing they exist.

"It's like a passport for SF's hidden vertical streets."

Every stairway is a photo opportunity, a neighborhood, a story. This app is the tool I wanted but couldn't find, so I built it.
